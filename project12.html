Physical Signs: 2025

Role:

Lead Graphic Display Designer

Date:

Designed & used throughout the year of 2025.

Context:

Made for Mile High Card Company as their Graphic Design & Social Media Director.

Tools:

Adobe Photoshop

Description:

  Throughout 2025, I designed and created a variety of signs tailored for card shows and expos, focusing on designs that could quickly capture attention in busy environments! I emphasized bright, card-themed visuals and clear messaging so each piece was easy to understand in just a glance. By keeping branding consistent while exploring a range of sizes, the signs felt cohesive and made the booth look professional. The signs helped draw more visitors & consignors to our booth and strengthened the company's presence at each show.
