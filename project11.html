Digital Advertisements: 2025

Role:

Lead Digital Advertising & Motion Designer

Date:

Throughout the year of 2025.

Context:

Made for Mile High Card Company as their Graphic Design & Social Media Director.

Tools:

Adobe Photoshop

Description:

  These advertisements were showcased across commonly used industry websites, connecting the company brand with a highly engaged community of collectors and enthusiasts through visually striking, animated GIF campaigns. I began by establishing a cohesive brand identity, which I continuously refined throughout the year to ensure consistency across all past and future campaigns. Each banner was created in Adobe Photoshop, where I incorporated motion design techniques to animate text and imagery, creating dynamic visuals that captured attention and enhanced engagement!
