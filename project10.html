Print Advertisements: 2025

Role:

Lead Print & Editorial Designer

Date:

Throughout the year of 2025.

Context:

Made for Mile High Card Company as their Graphic Design & Social Media Director.

Tools:

Adobe InDesign & Adobe Photoshop

Description:

  These ads were prominently featured in industry leading publications such as Beckett Vintage Collectibles and Sports Collector's Digest, allowing our company to reach a highly engaged audience of collectors and enthusiasts using beautifully designed print advertisements. I spent my time intially developing a strong, cohesive brand identity and a flexible design template, which I refined and used consistently throughout the year to maintain visual continuity across campaigns. I retouched images and icons as needed in Adobe Photoshop, but the core of the design process took place in Adobe InDesign, where I carefully crafted each layout and ensured every file was precisely prepared for high-quality print production.
