Catalogs: A Year of Iteration, 2025

Role:

Lead Project Manager, Print Designer, and Editorial Designer

Date:

  • Catalog 1: April 2025
  • Catalog 2: September 2025
  • Catalog 3: January 2025

    • Context:

    Working for Mile High Card Company as their Graphic Design & Social Media Director.

    Tools:

    Adobe InDesign & Adobe Photoshop

    Description:

      For our 2025 Auction Year, I designed and produced our physical catalogs from the ground up, overseeing everything from visual direction to print execution. Working in InDesign, I curated imagery, explored dynamic page compositions, and shaped each spread to feel intentional, balanced, and visually compelling. I also learned and mastered the art of using the "Data Merge" function to import large sets of text and information. I refreshed the typography and layout from the previous edition, establishing a more clear brand identity by choosing a standardized set of fonts and spacing. The refresh on the design has been met with strong positive feedback, with many noting the noticeable improvement in polish and overall aesthetic.
